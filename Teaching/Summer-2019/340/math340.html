Course description

This course discusses linear algebra with emphasis on theory and proofs. The central objects of study are finite-dimensional vector spaces and linear transformations between them. Topics include linear independence, bases, null space and range, matrices, duality, eigenvalues, inner products, the spectral theorem for normal operators, and the Jordan canonical form.
