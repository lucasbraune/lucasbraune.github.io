Grades

The course grade will be computed based on weekly homework, one midterm exam and one final exam. It will be computed as follows:

Homework (due Fridays) 25%
Midterm Exam (July 22) 30%
Final Exam (Aug 23) 45%

The two exams will take place on the indicated dates at the same time and place where lectures meet.

Grades will be published on Catalyst.

Exam policies

Students may bring a note sheet to exams, which must be letter-size and handwritten. Both sides are OK.

Make-up exams will not be given. If you miss an exam due to unavoidable, compelling, and well-documented circumstances, your final exam will be weighted more heavily. In case of observance of religious holidays or participation in university-sponsored activities, please contact me at least one week in advance. You will be asked to provide documentation for your absence.
