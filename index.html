About me

I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Washington's Department of Mathematics. I obtained my PhD at IMPA, and have also spent time at the mathematics departments of the Leibniz Universität Hannover, the Humboldt Universität-Berlin, the University of Cambridge and PUC-Rio. You can find my CV here.

Research

I am an algebraic geometer. My research interests include rationality questions, singularities of maps in positive characteristic, moduli of curves, and the minimal model program.

Research articles:

  • Critical loci and second-order singularities in arbitrary characteristic. Eprint: arXiv:1910.12176
  • (with K. Ascher and A. Turchet) The Erdős-Ulam Problem, Lang's conjecture, and uniformity. Submitted for publication. Eprint: arXiv:1901.02616

Other writings:

  • Irrational complete intersections. IMPA PhD Thesis, 2017. Eprint: arXiv:1909.05723
  • The Walter-Bertini theorem via jet schemes. Expository notes. PDF
  • Notes on normal crossing divisors. Expository notes. PDF
  • Branched covers of cones and Kodaira dimension. Expository notes. PDF
